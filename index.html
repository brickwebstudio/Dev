🌙
Night Mode Always — Dark theme on every app. Blue light accelerates myopia progression.
📏

💧
Eye Drops — Lubricating drops morning, midday, after work, before sleep. Never skip.
💡
Room Lighting — Never code in a dark room. Ambient light should match screen brightness.
😴
Sleep is Medicine — Your eyes physically repair during sleep. 5.5+ hrs minimum. Non-negotiable.
🤲
Palming (Morning) — Rub palms warm, cup over eyes for 30 sec. Reduces tension significantly.
